Pengling Sledding

Events

Regular events are held to see who has the courage to try this amazing sport! Just go to the slopes of the pirate laden Tiragarde Sound or the Witch infested peaks of Drustvar to partake in one!

Training

There is no real pengling sledding training. If you can do it, you live. If you can't... well, the yeti gets you. Good luck!

Why?

You might be wondering, "Why do I want to go sledding on a pengling?" What you should be wondering is, "Why do I not want to go sledding on a pengling?" It's the best experience you'll ever have! Especially while a giant, bloodthirsty yeti is chasing you!

Everything is provided for you!

All you need to participate in pengling sledding is a willingness to ride a pengling and a sense of self-preservation. The penglings are more than happy to provide their services and the bloodthirsty yeti instills a sense of danger in almost everyone who comes to one of our locations.

We simply request that you fill out a few forms accepting the immediate danger to your person. We are not at all liable for any harm that may or may not befall you while you are utilizing our service.
